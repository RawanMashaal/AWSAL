Awsal

aims to leverage technology to become the leading agricultural supply chain platform in the Middle East and North Africa region, by changing the way fresh goods are obtained, transported and sold. Creating new and more sustainable diets through technology to change the lives of Farmers, businesses and consumers by creating broad bridges between them.



Awsal's Role With Farmer

Awsal works closely with a range of Farmers. Our goal is to empower justice Agricultural sources, because we enable our Farmers to increase their income and Their quality of life.

When agricultural crops grow well, it is time to harvest. We supervise all process from A to Z and provide them with fertilizer and some agricultural materials for quality crops. Awsal gives them a card that they can use for shopping.





Awsal's Role With Customer

Awsal works closely with suppliers and offers our customers a wide range of crops all year round. After checking the quality, type and packaging of the product, Awsal starts shipping with refrigerated trucks and hits the road! Awsal is a fresh food delivery service for restaurants, hotels, retailers and manufacturers, mainly due to the following reasons:
Convenience: Offering our clients a large variety of fresh produce, both local and imported.
Competitive Prices: We offer a new facility at affordable prices and an easy way to pay with Awsal card which offers discounts on all purchases.
Reliability: Through our operational efficiency, you will receive your order on time and in-full.


Awsal Team

We are a group of people with one goal - to solve some agricultural problems, improve the financial management of all our suppliers and customers, and ensure that there are high productivity and economic activities that save money and time .
